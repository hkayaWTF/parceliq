01

Engineering and connecting 20+ proprietary and public data sources — FEMA, EPA, municipal zoning databases, county assessors, market data providers, satellite imagery — into a single unified data pipeline with redundancy and caching.

Months 2–4

Building the orchestration layer that dispatches multiple simultaneous research jobs per property request — each one querying different data sources, processing results, and returning structured outputs — all completing within 15 minutes total.
